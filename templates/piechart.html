A pie chart is a circular statistical graphic divided into slices to illustrate numerical proportion.

What is a Pie Chart?

A Pie Chart uses sectors to show parts of a whole or proportions of different categories.
Each slice represents a category and the size of the slice is proportional to the quantity it represents.

How to Read a Pie Chart?

In a pie chart, each slice's size represents the proportion of the whole data set.
It's used to visualize and compare proportions or percentages easily.
